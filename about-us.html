Who are we?

Froots is an online grocery store that has a goal of making healthy living accessible and affordable for all south africans. The fruit is sourced from our hydroponic urban farms that occupy 50% less space required by most farms and are 80% more efficient. We use very little water and electricity to through our solar panels and hydroponic systems to provide great quality fruit for half the normal price.

How it all began...

Froots was founded by a group of 5 agricultural students from the University of the Witwatersrand and saw the need for students to adopt healthier eating habits. Most students would live off of two minute noodles and fast food because of the lack of funds to fill their stomachs with heathier food.

So Froots was founded by students who built a roof-top hydroponic farm and began selling fruit to students in the school however branched out to the whole of South Africa!
